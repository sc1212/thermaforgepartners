# ThermaForge Partners — brand spec

> **Working draft. Built by Carson Systems for ThermaForge LLC. Zach Rains is the founding face of the brand. Final brand revisions to be approved by Zach before public DNS at thermaforgepartners.com.**

## What ThermaForge is

ThermaForge Partners is a fractional operating-partner firm for HVAC owners doing $5M+ in
revenue who are scaling toward a near-term recap, sale, or generational succession.

It is **a separate LLC**, not a Carson Systems product. ThermaForge engagements deploy a
Carson Systems platform stack (Routebook ERP, optionally Frontline AI, optionally the
Carson Systems content engine + Echo Clip video) under a non-exclusive license from
Carson Systems LLC.

The two firms are **partners, not parent/child**.

## Voice persona

Operator-to-owner. The audience is HVAC owners who built a $5M–$25M business by being
the best operator in town and who are now thinking about what comes next — bigger team,
recap, sale, succession, leaving the truck for the office. Speak to that owner the way an
ops veteran would in their kitchen on a Sunday afternoon.

- Direct, plainspoken, specific.
- Quote actual HVAC operational pain — recall, callbacks, AR over 60, tech turnover,
  recruiting in a tight labor market, the cost of a bad dispatch.
- Never enterprise-SaaS-bro language. No "leverage," "synergy," "AI-powered," "journey,"
  "transform," "elevate," "unlock."
- Use the language of EBITDA, multiples, recap, succession, GP/NP, callback rate,
  first-time-fix, technician utilization. Owners doing $5M+ already speak this language.
- Never UPPERCASE the wordmark when it's in body copy — only in the lockup.

## Visual identity

### Color (different from Routebook on purpose)

| | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Ember** (primary) | `#E8602B` | Wordmark accent, CTAs, primary highlights — the heat of a forge |
| **Deep charcoal** (surface) | `#0D0D0F` | Primary background, near-black, cooler than Routebook's #1A1A1A |
| **Off-white** (text on dark) | `#F5F5F0` | Body text on dark, cards on light |
| **Steel** (secondary) | `#4A5058` | Secondary borders, dividers, dim text |
| **Mist** (tertiary text on dark) | `#9A9DA3` | Captions, eyebrows on dark surfaces |
| **Hairline** | `rgba(245,245,240,0.08)` | Section dividers on dark |

**Forbidden colors:**
- Carson Systems blue `#3b82f6` — that is the parent-brand color.
- Plymouth orange `#F4A015` — Plymouth's mark, would confuse the family.
- Routebook copper `#C97B47` — Routebook's mark. ThermaForge runs hotter and darker.

### Typography

- **Inter** for everything. Bold for the wordmark and section headlines, regular for body.
- Wordmark is `THERMAFORGE PARTNERS` — UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.5px, weight 700.
  Only in the lockup; in body copy use sentence case ("ThermaForge Partners").
- Body: 16px on mobile, 17px on desktop. Tight leading (1.5).

### Mark

No standalone mark in v1. Wordmark + accent ember square.
A `★` in front of the wordmark or as a separator is acceptable as a temporary device
representing forge sparks. Zach can commission a proper TF monogram later.

### Surface design

- Dark-first design. Most sections sit on `#0D0D0F`.
- One light "bone" section breaks the dark for the engagement model — this is the
  "what we actually do" section that needs to read clearly.
- Hairline rules between sections. No heavy borders.
- Minimal use of orange — it should feel like ember glowing in a dark room, not a
  tropical sunset.
