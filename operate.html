Three hires, ramp risk on each, no built-in equity alignment.

EBITDA AT RECAP

One hundred basis points pays for the engagement four to six times over.

Take a $7.5M HVAC shop running 12% EBITDA — that's $900K EBITDA. PE multiples in this segment run 5x to 7x, putting recap valuation at $4.5M–$6.3M.
